An email relay is a service that allows you to set up a unique email address (called “alias”) that will forward all incoming emails to the email address of your choice. Share your relay address (email alias) instead of your personal address to keep it private. Additionally, you have the flexibility to disable the email alias at any point, protecting your inbox from any potential spam.

Using SimpleLogin as a preconfigured email relay adds an extra layer of privacy and security to your email workflow. You can create an alias for each website which routes messages to your real email address while still keeping it private.

Apple's Hide My Email is not a stand-alone product, but rather a feature of their iCloud service.
